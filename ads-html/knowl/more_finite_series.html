Example1.5.2More finite series

If the general terms in a series are more specific, the sum can often be simplified. For example,

  1. \(\sum _{i=1}^4 i^2=1^2+2^2+3^2+4^2=30\)
  2. \(\sum _{i=1}^5 (2i-1)=(2\ 1-1)+(2\ 2-1)+(2\ 3-1)+(2\ 4-1)+(2\ 5-1)\\ \\ \quad \quad =1+3+5+7+9\\ \\ \quad \quad =25\)
