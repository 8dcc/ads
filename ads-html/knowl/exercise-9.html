Let \(U= \{1, 2, 3, . . . , 9\}\). Give examples of sets \( A\), \( B\), and \( C\) for which:
  1. \(A\cap (B\cap C)=(A\cap B)\cap C\)
  2. \(A\cap (B\cup C)=(A\cap B)\cup (A\cap C)\)
  3. \((A \cup B)^c= A^c\cap B^c\)
  4. \(A \cup A^c = U\)
  5. \(A \subseteq A\cup B\)
  6. \(A\cap B \subseteq A\)
