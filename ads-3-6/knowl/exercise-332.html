  3. \(P(n) =\)\(\sum_{j=1}^n \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^j\text{,}\) \(n\geq 0\)

  4. \(T(n)= \sum_{j=1}^n j^3\text{,}\) \(n\geq 1\)
