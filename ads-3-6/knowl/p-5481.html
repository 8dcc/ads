It is quite easy to describe any of the dihedral groups in a similar fashion. Let \(r= (1,2, \ldots , n)\text{,}\) an \(n\)-cycle, and \(f= (1,n)(2,n-1)\ldots\) Then

\begin{equation*} D_n= \langle r,f\rangle = \left\{i, r, r^2, \ldots, r^{n-1}, f, r\circ f, r^2\circ f, \ldots, r^{n-1}\circ f\right\} \end{equation*}
