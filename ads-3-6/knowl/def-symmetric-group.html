Definition 15.3.5. Symmetric Group.

Let \(A\) be a nonempty set. The set of all permutations on \(A\) with the operation of function composition is called the symmetric group on A, denoted \(S_A\text{.}\)

The cardinality of a finite set \(A\) is more significant than the elements, and we will denote by \(S_n\) the symmetric group on any set of cardinality \(n\text{,}\) \(n\geq 1\text{.}\)
