Suppose that a list of nine items, \((r(l), r(2), . . . , r(9))\text{,}\) is sorted by key in decending order so that \(r(3).\texttt{key} = 12\) and \(r(4).\texttt{key} = 10\text{.}\) List the executions of the BinarySearch algorithms that would be needed to complete BinarySearch(1,9) when:

  1. The search key is C = 12

  2. The search key is C = 11

Assume that distinct items have distinct keys.
