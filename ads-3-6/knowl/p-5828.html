  1. Let \(R_1\text{,}\) \(R_2\text{,}\) \(\ldots\text{,}\) \(R_n\) be rings. Prove the multiplicative, associative, and distributive laws for the ring

    \begin{equation*} R=\underset{i=1}{\overset{n}{\times }}R_i \end{equation*}

  2. If each of the \(R_i\) is commutative, is R commutative?

  3. Under what conditions will \(R\) be a ring with unity?

  4. What will the units of \(R\) be when it has a unity?
