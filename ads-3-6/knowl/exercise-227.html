  1. List all elements in \(rs\text{.}\)

  2. List all elements in \(sr\text{.}\)

  3. Illustrate \(rs\) and \(sr\) via a diagram.

  4. Is the relation \(rs\) equal to the relation \(sr\text{?}\)
