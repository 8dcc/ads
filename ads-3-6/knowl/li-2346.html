Item 4.

Production rules: \(I \to \alpha\text{,}\) where \(\alpha\) is any letter, \(I \to \alpha+X\) for any letter \(\alpha\text{,}\) \(X\to X+\beta\) for any letter, digit or underscore, \(\beta\text{,}\) and \(X \to \beta\) for any letter, digit or underscore, \(\beta\text{.}\) There are a total of \(52+52+63+63=230\) production rules for this grammar. The language \(L(G)\) consists of all valid SageMath variable names.
