From Chapter 11 we know that \(P\) is an abelian group under the operation of componentwise addition:

\begin{equation*} a + b = \left(a_1 +_1 b_1, a_2 +_2 b_2 , . . . , a_n +_n b_n\right) \end{equation*}

We also define multiplication on \(P\) componentwise:

\begin{equation*} a \cdot b = \left(a_1 \cdot_1 b_1, a_2 \cdot _2 b_2 , . . . , a_n \cdot_n b_n\right) \end{equation*}
