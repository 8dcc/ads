First we ask ourselves: Is the matrix \(A =\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \\ \end{array} \right)\) equal to the matrix \(B =\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 5 \\ \end{array} \right)\text{?}\) No, they are not because the corresponding entries in the second row, second column of the two matrices are not equal.
