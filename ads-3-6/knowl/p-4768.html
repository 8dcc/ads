Let \((L, \preceq)\) be a poset. If \(a, b \in L\text{,}\) then \(\ell \in L\) is a greatest lower bound of \(a\) and \(b\) if and only if

  • \(\ell \preceq a\)

  • \(\ell \preceq b\)

  • If \(\ell' \in L\) such that \(\ell' \preceq a\) and \(\ell' \preceq b\text{,}\) then \(\ell' \preceq \ell\text{.}\)
