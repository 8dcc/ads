Given power series

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{c} f(x)=\sum_{i=0}^{\infty } a_i x^i=a_0 + a_1 x+a_2 x^2+ \cdots\textrm{ and}\\ g(x)=\sum_{i=0}^{\infty } b_i x^i=b_0 + b_1 x+b_2 x^2+ \cdots\\ \end{array} \end{equation*}

their sum is

\begin{equation*} \begin{split} f(x)+g(x)&=\sum_{i=0}^{\infty }\left(a_i+b_i\right) x^i\\ &=(a_0 +a_1) + (a_1+b_1) x+(a_2+b_2) x^2+(a_3+b_3) x^3+ \cdots \\ \end{split}\text{.} \end{equation*}
