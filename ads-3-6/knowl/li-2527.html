Item 1.

\(a \cdot 0 = a \cdot(0 + 0) = a \cdot 0 + a \cdot 0\text{,}\) the last equality valid by the left distributive axiom. Hence if we add \(-(a \cdot 0)\) to both sides of the equality above, we obtain \(a \cdot 0 = 0\text{.}\) Similarly, we can prove that \(0 \cdot a = 0\text{.}\)
