Now we establish the connection between the operations on sequences and generating functions. Let \(S\) and \(T\) be sequences and let \(c\) be a real number.

\begin{gather} G(S+T;z)=G(S;z)+G(T;z)\tag{8.5.13}\\ G(cS;z)=c G(S;z)\tag{8.5.14}\\ G(S*T;z)=G(S;z) G(T;z)\tag{8.5.15}\\ G(S\uparrow ;z)=(G(S;z)-S(0))/z\tag{8.5.16}\\ G(S\downarrow ;z)=z G(S;z)\tag{8.5.17} \end{gather}
