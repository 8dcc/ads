Example 15.4.8. A non-normal subgroup.

The right cosets of \(\left\langle f_1\right\rangle \leq S_3\) are \(\left\{i, f_1\right\}\text{,}\) \(\left\{r_1 f_2 \right\}\text{,}\) and \(\left\{r_2 ,f_3\right\}\text{.}\) These are not the same as the left cosets of \(\left\langle f_1\right\rangle\text{.}\) In addition, \(f_2{}^{-1}f_1f_2=f_2f_1f_2=f_3\notin \left\langle f_1\right\rangle\text{.}\) Thus, \(\left\langle f_1\right\rangle\) is not normal.
