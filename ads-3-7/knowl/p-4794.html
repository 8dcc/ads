Let \((L, \preceq)\) be a poset. If \(a, b \in L\text{,}\) then \(u \in L\) is a least upper bound of \(a\) and \(b\) if and only if

  • \(a \preceq u\)

  • \(b \preceq u\)

  • If \(u' \in L\) such that if \(a \preceq u'\) and \(b \preceq u'\text{,}\) then \(u \preceq u'\text{.}\)
