Item 1.

The first step in the diagonalization process is the determination of the eigenvalues. The ordering of the eigenvalues is purely arbitrary. If we designate \(\lambda_1 = 4\) and \(\lambda_2=1\text{,}\) the columns of \(P\) would be interchanged and \(D\) would be \(\left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \\ \end{array} \right)\) (see Exercise 3b of this section). Nonetheless, the final outcome of the application to which we are applying the diagonalization process would be the same.
