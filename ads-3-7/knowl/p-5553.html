Although \(D_4\) isn't cyclic (since it isn't abelian), it can be generated from the two elements \(r_1\) and \(f_1\text{:}\)

\begin{equation*} D_4= \left\langle r_1,f_1\right\rangle = \left\{i, r_1, r_1{}^2, r_1{}^3, f_1, r_1\circ f_1, r_1{}^2\circ f_1, r_1{}^3\circ f_1\right\} \end{equation*}
