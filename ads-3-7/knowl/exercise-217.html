Exercise 4.

Let \(A =\left( \begin{array}{cc} 7 & 4 \\ 2 & 1 \\ \end{array} \right)\) and \(B =\left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 5 \\ 2 & 4 \\ \end{array} \right)\text{.}\) Compute the following as efficiently as possible by using any of the Laws of Matrix Algebra:

  1. \(A B\)

  2. \(A + B\)

  3. \(A^2 + A B + B A + B ^2\)

  4. \(B^{-1}A^{-1}\)

  5. \(A^2 + A B\)
