This relation is symmetric. We can prove this through the following chain of implications.

\begin{equation*} \begin{split} a \equiv_m b &\Rightarrow m \mid (a-b)\\ & \Rightarrow \textrm{For some } k \in \mathbb{Z}, a-b = m k \\ & \Rightarrow b-a = m (-k)\\ & \Rightarrow m \mid (b-a)\\ & \Rightarrow b \equiv_m a \end{split}\text{.} \end{equation*}
