Item 3.5.4.3.d.

Direct proof:

  1. \(p\to q\)

  2. \(q\to r\)

  3. \(p\to r\)

  4. \(p\lor r\)

  5. \(\neg p\lor r\)

  6. \((p\lor r)\land (\neg p\lor r)\)

  7. \((p\land \neg p)\lor r\)

  8. \(0\lor r\)

  9. \(r\)\(\square\)

Indirect proof:

  1. \(\neg r\) Negated conclusion

  2. \(p\lor r\) Premise

  3. \(p\) (1), (2)

  4. \(p\to q\) Premise

  5. \(q \quad \) Detachment (3), (4)

  6. \(q\to r\) Premise

  7. \(r \quad \)Detachment (5), (6)

  8. 0 (1), (7) \(\square\)
