Extended Rule Of Products. The rule of products can be extended to include sequences of more than two operations. If \(n\) operations must be performed, and the number of options for each operation is \(p_1\text{,}\) \(p_2, \dots, p_n\) respectively, with each \(p_i\) independent of previous choices, then the \(n\) operations can be performed \(p_1 \cdot p_2 \cdot \cdots \cdot p_n\) different ways.
