Let \(T_0\) be the final effect (state and output) on the parity checker of the input 0. Similarly, \(T_1\) is defined as the final effect on the parity checker of the input 1. More precisely,

\begin{equation*} T_0(\textrm{ even})=(\textrm{ even},0) \quad \textrm{and} \quad T_0(\textrm{ odd})=(\textrm{ odd},1)\textrm{,} \end{equation*}

while

\begin{equation*} T_1(\textrm{ even})=(\textrm{ odd},1) \quad \textrm{and} \quad T_1(\textrm{ odd})=(\textrm{ even},0)\textrm{.} \end{equation*}
