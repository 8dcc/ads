Theorem 15.4.6.

If \(H \leq G\text{,}\) then the operation induced on left cosets of \(H\) by the operation of \(G\) is well defined if and only if any one of the following conditions is true:

  1. \(H\) is a normal subgroup of \(G\text{.}\)

  2. If \(h \in H\text{,}\) \(a \in G\text{,}\) then there exists \(h' \in H\) such that \(h*a = a*h'\text{.}\)

  3. If \(h \in H\text{,}\) \(a \in G\text{,}\) then \(a^{-1}*h*a \in H\text{.}\)
