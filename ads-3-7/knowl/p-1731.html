Let \(A =\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 3 & 2 \\ -5 & 1 \\ \end{array} \right)\text{,}\) a \(3\times 2\) matrix, and let \(B =\left( \begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 1 \\ \end{array} \right)\text{,}\) a \(2\times 1\) matrix. Then \(A B\) is a \(3 \times 1\) matrix:

\begin{equation*} A B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 3 & 2 \\ -5 & 1 \\ \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 1 \\ \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 1\cdot 6+0\cdot 1 \\ 3 \cdot 6 + 2\cdot 1 \\ -5 \cdot 6 + 1\cdot 1 \\ \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 20 \\ -29 \\ \end{array} \right) \end{equation*}
