The input alphabet is \(B=\{0,1\}\) and the output alphabet is also \(B\text{.}\) The state set is \(\{even, odd\}\text{.}\) The following table defines the output and next-state functions.
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{c|ccc} x & s & w(x,s) & t(x,s) \\ \hline \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ \end{array} & \begin{array}{c} \textrm{ even} \\ \textrm{ odd} \\ \textrm{ even} \\ \textrm{ odd} \\ \end{array} & \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 1 \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ \end{array} & \begin{array}{c} \textrm{ even} \\ \textrm{ odd} \\ \textrm{ odd} \\ \textrm{ even} \\ \end{array} \\ \end{array} \end{equation*}
