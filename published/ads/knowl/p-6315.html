Determine the splitting field of \(f(x) = x^2+ 1\) over \(\mathbb{R}\text{.}\) This means consider the polynomial \(f(x) = x^2+1 \in \mathbb{R}[x]\) and find the smallest field that contains \(\mathbb{R}\) and all the zeros of \(f(x)\text{.}\) Denote this field by \(\mathbb{R}(i)\text{.}\)
