Many of the basic formulas in calculus are true in matrix calculus. For example,
\begin{equation*} \frac{d (A(t)+B(t))}{d t} = \frac{d A(t)}{d t}+ \frac{d B(t)}{d t} \end{equation*}
and if \(A\) is a constant matrix,
\begin{equation*} \frac{d e^{A t}}{d t}= A e^{A t} \end{equation*}
