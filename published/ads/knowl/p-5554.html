The group of positive integers modulo 11 with modulo 11 multiplication, \([\mathbb{U}_{11} ;\times_{11}]\text{,}\) is cyclic. One of its generators is 6: \(6^1 = 6\text{,}\) \(6^2 = 3\text{,}\) \(6^3= 7\text{,}\)\(\ldots\) , \(6^9=2\text{,}\) and \(6^{10}=1\text{,}\) the identity of the group.
