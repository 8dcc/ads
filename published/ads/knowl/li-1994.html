Item b.
We call the operations in a direct product componentwise operations, and they are indeed operations on \(V\text{.}\) If two \(n\)-tuples, \(a\) and \(b\text{,}\) are selected from \(V\text{,}\) the first components of \(a\) and \(b\text{,}\) \(a_1\) and \(b_1\text{,}\) are operated on with \(*_1\) to obtain \(a_1*_1b_1\text{,}\) the first component of \(a * b\text{.}\) Note that since \(*_1\) is an operation on \(V_1\text{,}\) \(a_1*_1b_1\) is an element of \(V_1\text{.}\) Similarly, all other components of \(a * b\text{,}\) as they are defined, belong to their proper sets.
