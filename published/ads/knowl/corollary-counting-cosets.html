Corollary 15.2.9. A Coset Counting Formula.

If \(\lvert G\rvert < \infty\) and \(H\leq G\text{,}\) the number of distinct left cosets of \(H\) equals \(\frac{\lvert G\rvert }{\lvert H\rvert }\text{.}\) For this reason we use \(G/H\) to denote the set of left cosets of \(H\) in \(G\)
