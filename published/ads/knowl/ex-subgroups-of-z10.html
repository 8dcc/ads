Example 15.1.11. All subgroups of \(\mathbb{Z}_{10}\).

The only proper subgroups of \(\mathbb{Z}_{10}\) are \(H_1 = \{0, 5\}\) and \(H_2 = \{0, 2, 4, 6, 8\}\text{.}\) They are both cyclic: \(H_1= \langle 5 \rangle\text{,}\) while \(H_2 = \langle 2 \rangle = \langle 4 \rangle = \langle 6 \rangle = \langle 8 \rangle\text{.}\) The generators of \(\mathbb{Z}_{10}\) are 1, 3, 7, and 9.
