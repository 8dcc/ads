Exercise 6.

Diagonalize the following, if possible:
  1. \(\displaystyle \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 \\ \end{array} \right)\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ 4 & 2 \\ \end{array} \right)\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 \\ \end{array} \right)\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \left( \begin{array}{ccc} 1 & 3 & 6 \\ -3 & -5 & -6 \\ 3 & 3 & 6 \\ \end{array} \right)\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \left( \begin{array}{ccc} 1 & 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 1 \\ \end{array} \right)\)
  6. \(\displaystyle \left( \begin{array}{ccc} 2 & -1 & 0 \\ -1 & 2 & -1 \\ 0 & -1 & 2 \\ \end{array} \right)\)
