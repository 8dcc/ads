Exercise 2.

Which of the following functions are homomorphisms? What are the kernels of those functions that are homomorphisms?
  1. \(\alpha_1: M_{2\times 2}(\mathbb{R}) \rightarrow \mathbb{R}\text{,}\) defined by \(\alpha_1(A) = A_{11} A_{22} + A_{12} A_{21}\text{.}\)
  2. \(\alpha_2 : \left(\mathbb{R}^*\right)^2 \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^*\) defined by \(\alpha_2 (a, b) = a b\text{.}\)
  3. \(\alpha_3 : \left\{\left.A \in M_{2\times 2}(\mathbb{R}) \right| \det A \neq 0\right\} \to \mathbb{R}^*\text{,}\) where \(\alpha_3(A) = \det A\text{.}\)
  4. \(\alpha_4 : S_4\rightarrow S_4\) defined by \(\alpha_4(f)=f^{-1}\text{.}\)
