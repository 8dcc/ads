Example 16.1.19.

We will compute \(2 \cdot (-2)\) in the ring \(\left[\mathbb{Z}_6;+_6,\times_6\right]\text{.}\) \(2 \times_6 (-2) = -\left(2\times_6 2\right)= -4 = 2\text{,}\) since the additive inverse of 4 (mod 6) is 2. Of course, we could have done the calculation directly as \(2 \times_6 (-2) = 2 \times_6 4 = 2\)
