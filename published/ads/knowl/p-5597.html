In order to get the true sum from our scheme, the modulus would need to be increased by moving from 21600 to, for example, \(21600*23=496800\text{.}\) Mapping into the new group, \(G=\mathbb{Z}_{32}\times \mathbb{Z}_{27}\times \mathbb{Z}_{25}\times \mathbb{Z}_{23}\) will take slightly longer, as will the inversion process with crt, but adding the summands that are in the form of quadruples can be done with no additional time.
